## ADDITIONAL NOTES FOR DESIGNER

### Tone Guidance

- **Professional but approachable** — We're coaches, not salespeople
- **Motivating and action-oriented** — Focus on what agents can achieve
- **Confident without being pushy** — Let the value speak for itself
- **Direct address** — Use "you" and "your" throughout
- **Outcome-focused** — Emphasize what they'll get, not just what we offer

### Key Phrases to Incorporate (Brand Language)

- "Design a career and life worth smiling about" (trademark phrase)
- "Next Level" (Power Agent terminology)
- "Breakthrough year"
- "Know your numbers"
- "Daily activities"
- "GCI goals"

### Visual Suggestions

- Navy dark (#0F172A, #1E293B) for headers and primary elements
- Clean white content areas
- Professional real estate imagery or Darryl Davis photography
- Consider showing app screenshots in the section preview area
- Icons for the benefit cards and how-it-works steps

### Mobile Considerations

- Headlines should work on small screens (keep them punchy)
- CTAs should be thumb-friendly
- Section previews may need to stack into a carousel on mobile
